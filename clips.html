Project

Use Apple Clips to explain steps in a process such as how to bake cookies, how to change a tire, or for steps you would take to have a successful vacation. Your Clips video should include images, video, text, and audio recordings. Try to also include some of the unique Clips features like live titles, filters, overlays, posters, and music.
