Pros

Cons

  • Requires lots of bandwidth if an entire class is using at the same time

What is Edpuzzle?

Edpuzzle is an app that allows you to add questions directly to a video. It is an easy and effective way to deliver videos in the classroom and check for understanding. With Edpuzzle, video comes to life with audio-notes and questions popping up as the video plays. Students can download the app and opt-in to receive video-assignments directly with questions.

What is set-up like?

The teacher signs up for an account at https://edpuzzle.com/. The teacher can add content from Edpuzzle, Youtube, or upload from their computer and assign it to a class. Add multiple choice or open ended questions to the video by clicking where you want it to show in the timeline.

What type of data feedback is available?

Students download the Edpuzzle app and use the code to join a class and access the video lessons. Students watch the video at their own pace. As they submit their answers, the data becomes available to the teacher by clicking on the video in the "My Content" section of the teacher dashboard.
