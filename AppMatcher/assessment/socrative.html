What is Socrative?

Quickly assess students with prepared activities or on-the-fly questions to get immediate insight into student understanding. Then use auto-populated results to determine the best instructional approach to most effectively drive learning. Socrative includes rich data reports on individual and class responses to questions.

What is set-up like?

Go to www.socrative.com or download the Socrative teacher app to set up an account. Students will need to download the Socrative student app. Socrative includes a quiz option for pre-planned assessments, or it includes a quick question/answer and an exit ticket option with no need to prepare beforehand. Launch a quiz, receive exit tickets, or ask a quick question for instant student feedback. Socrative also has a Space Race option for group competitions. The teacher launches the Socrative teacher app and chooses the activity. Students launch the Socrative Student app and enter the room code given by the teacher. Students submit answers to the questions generated by the teacher. When using the Quick Question option, students can create the questions for the class to answer.

What type of data feedback is available?

Review student understanding at the class, individual student, or question-level. Each Socrative report is saved to your account so you can quickly download, email, or transfer reports to Google Drive.
