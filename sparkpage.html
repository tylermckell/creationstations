Project

Use Adobe Spark Page to create a webpage about a place you have visited or would like to visit. Your page should include:

  • Photos and video
  • A description of the location using text
  • A quote about the location
  • Images in the various formats (Inline, Full Screen, Window, and Full Width)
  • A Glideshow with images and text
  • A button that links to a visitors website

Bonus Items:

  • A YouTube video about the location
  • A map showing the location

Materials

A device with the Adobe Spark Page app. The Adobe Spark apps can also be found online at spark.adobe.com
